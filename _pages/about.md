I'm Michel Carvalho, a machine learning researcher and engineer interested in the theory and practice of modern ML systems.

My main interests lie at the intersection of **deep learning**, **probabilistic models**, and **mathematical foundations of AI**. I enjoy understanding *why* things work — not just *that* they work.

On this site I write about ML topics I find interesting or am currently studying: from attention mechanisms and transformers to diffusion models, optimization theory, and beyond. Posts aim to be precise and self-contained, with math when needed.

Feel free to reach out if you want to discuss any of the topics here.
